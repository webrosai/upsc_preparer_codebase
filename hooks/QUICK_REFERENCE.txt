❌ ERROR: "Invalid login credentials"
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Cause:    Email not confirmed yet
Solution: 
1. Check email inbox (including spam folder)
2. Click confirmation link
3. Try signing in again

❌ ERROR: "User already registered"
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Cause:    Email already has account
Solution:
1. Use different email to sign up, OR
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset existing account

❌ ERROR: "Confirmation email not received"
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Check:
1. Spam/Junk folder
2. Email entered correctly during signup
3. Wait 5 minutes for delivery
Try:
1. Sign up again with correct email
2. Check email again
